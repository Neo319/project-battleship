JS Battleship

This application was made during The Odin project's Battleship Project.

Creator: Alex N on Github.

Instructions:

In Battleship, players try to locate enemy ships by shooting shots at sectors in a 10 x 10 grid before the opponent locates and takes down your ships.

Start by using the "Generate New Layout" Button to position your ships, then use the "Start" button to begin a game. Click on the Computer's Gameboard (the upper square) to input an attack. Play against a simple computer that uses random moves. You can press "Reset" to begin a new game at any time.
